__Geometric Algebra HW 5 (Cramer's Rule)__   
__MultiV 2021-22 / Dr. Kessner__    

\vspace{.25in}

Solve the following systems of equations using Cramer's Rule.  Check your
answers.

\vspace{.25in}

1.    
$\begin{aligned}
x + 3y &= 7 \\
5x + 7y &= 19
\end{aligned}$

\vspace{.25in}

2.
$\begin{aligned}
x + 3y &= -2 \\
5x + 7y &= -2 
\end{aligned}$

\vspace{.25in}


3.
$\begin{aligned}
-x + z &= 2 \\
-y + z &= 1 \\ 
-x -y + z &= 0 \\ 
\end{aligned}$
